Dynamic Elbow Brace

We've reimagined the traditional elbow brace by creating a dynamic, inflatable support that offers personalized pressure without sacrificing mobility. This device uses inflation to automatically contract, providing targeted support exactly where you need it. Unlike static bandages, users can precisely regulate the air pressure at any time, eliminating the need for constant re-wrapping. Through rapid inflation and deflation, it can also deliver dynamic compression therapy on the go.

The true innovation lies in its Kirigami-inspired structure. While traditional rigid or simple inflatable braces often restrict movement, our design ensures the elbow's full range of motion remains free, allowing for natural and unrestricted bending.

Perfect for sports training, rehabilitation, and injury prevention, this brace allows for real-time pressure adjustments to balance protection with performance, effectively helping prevent overuse injuries during prolonged use.
